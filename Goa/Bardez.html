Bardez

  1. Overview : Bardez was a concelho (county) in the Portuguese era's possessions of Goa, Daman and Diu. Situated in the northern portion of the present-day Goa state, in the Konkan region of India, today it is co-terminus and is an eponymous taluka (sub-district) of the North Goa district.

  2. Villages and Towns in Bardez : List of towns in Bardez taluka : Mapusa, Penha-de-Franca, Calangute, Socorro, Siolim, Anjuna, Candolim, Reis Magos, Salvador do Mundo, Saligao, Aldona, Pilerne, Nerul, Guirim, Colvale, Moira

    List of villages in Bardez taluka : Arpora, Assagao, Assonora, Bastora, Calvim, Camurlim, Canca, Corjuem, Marna, Marra, Moitem, Nachinola, Nadora, Nagoa, Olaulim, Oxel, Paliem, Parra, Pirna, Pomburpa, Ponolem, Punola, Revora, Sangolda, Sircaim, Tivim, Ucassaim, Verla

  5. Geology : Bardez is delimited on the north by the Chapora River, on the south by the Mandovi River, on the east by the Mapusa River, which originates in Bardez itself near the capital city of Mapusa, and on the west by the Arabian Sea.

  6. Culture and Religion : Among the Goan Catholics, it is famous as the birthplace of Venerable Father Agnelo, who is expected to attain sainthood. He is one of the two most prominent Catholic religious Goan, the other being St. Saint Joseph Vaz, the 'Apostle of Ceylon', who attained sainthood recently.

  7. Places to Visit :
    - Major Temples : Shanta Durga Temple, Shri Ram Devsthan Temple, Shree Swami Achyutanand Temple, Shree Sakhaleshwar Prasann Temple, Shri Jambhleshwar Prasanna Temple, Shree Rumdeshwar Vetal Temple
    - Major Mosques : Eidgah Masjid,Mapusa, Masjid E Hamza, Usmaniya Markaz Masjid
    - Major Churches : St. Cajetan Church, St. Francis Xavier’s Church, Our Lady of Lourdes Chape, St. Rita's Church, St. Francis of Assisi Church, St. Christopher’s Church
    - Forts : Chapora Fort, Sinquerim Fort, Aguada Fort, Reis Magos Fort, Colvale Fort, Fort Tivim
    - Rivers : Mandovi River, Mapusa River, Chapora River
    - Waterfalls : Sirsai waterfall, Saish waterfal
    - Beaches : Chapora Beach, Vagator Beach Cross, Anjuna Beach, Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Candolim Beach, Sinquerim Beach, Onyx Beach
