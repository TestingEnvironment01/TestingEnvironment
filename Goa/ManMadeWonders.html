Man Made Wonders

Pernem

- Major Temples : Mauli Temple area at Sarmale and at Mulvir Temple are some of the many temples in Pernem.
- Major Mosques : Khwaja Masjid and Dargah is located at the bay of Terekhol River
- Major Churches : St. Joseph Church, St.Sebastian Chapel, Naibag Pernem, Goa, Mount Carmel Church are some of the many churches in Pernem.
- Forts : Alorna Fort, Tiracol rocks beach

Bardez

- Major Temples : Shanta Durga Temple, Shri Ram Devsthan Temple, Shree Swami Achyutanand Temple, Shree Sakhaleshwar Prasann Temple, Shri Jambhleshwar Prasanna Temple, Shree Rumdeshwar Vetal Temple
- Major Mosques : Eidgah Masjid,Mapusa, Masjid E Hamza, Usmaniya Markaz Masjid
- Major Churches : St. Cajetan Church, St. Francis Xavier’s Church, Our Lady of Lourdes Chape, St. Rita's Church, St. Francis of Assisi Church, St. Christopher’s Church
- Forts : Chapora Fort, Sinquerim Fort, Aguada Fort, Reis Magos Fort, Colvale Fort, Fort Tivim

Bicholim

- Major Temples : Shantadurga Temple, Shri Yogeshwari Devasthaan, Koteshwar Temple, Saibaba Temple - Lakhere Bicholim, Shree Manguesh Temple, Shri Mahamaya Temple, Siddhivinayak Temple, Shri Sateri Temple
- Major Mosques : Namazgah, Surla Mosque, Azad Jama Masjid
- Major Churches : Our Lady of Grace Church
- Forts : Maharaja Dipaji Rane's fort, Juve Fort

Sattari

- Major Temples : Shri Sateri Kelbai Temple, Vadadev Temple, Shree Ajoba temple, Shri Bhumika Mata Temple
- Major Mosques : Bhaismad Sunni Jamat Masjid, Sanquelim Jamia Masjid
- Major Churches : Our Lady of Lourdes Church, St. John of the Cross Church
- Forts : Nanuz Fort

Tiswadi

- Major Temples : Shree Mahalaxmi Temple, Hanuman Mandir, Shree Jagadamba Shashti Shantadurga Temple, Shri Gajantlaxmi Temple, Shiva Temple
- Major Mosques : Jama Mosque, Madani Masjid, Masjid-E-Inaam
- Major Churches : St. Anne's Church, Holy place old Goa church, Our Lady Of Pilar Church, Basilica of Bom Jesus
- Museums : Goa Science Centre & Planetarium, Goa State Museum, Archaeological Museum of Goa, Pilar seminary museum
- Gardens & Sanctuaries : Carambolim Lake Migratory Bird Sanctuary, Curca spring, Panjim Municipal Garden, Palmar Garden, Campal Gardens, Dr. Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary

Ponda

- Major Temples : Shree Nagesh Maharudra Mandir, Shree Ramnath Devsthan, Shankar Parvati Temple, Shree Shantadurga Mandir, Shri Ayappa Temple, Ponda Goa, Shree Bhagavati Temple, Shree Mahalaxmi Saunsthan, Bandora, Shree Kapileshwar Temple
- Major Mosques : Safa Masjid, Masjid E Noorani
- Major Churches : St Anne's Church, Mount Carmel Chapel
- Forts : Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Fort,
- Gardens : Khadpabandh Garden, Municipal Park
- Biodiversity Reserves / Sanctuary : Mystic Woods by The Butterfly Conservatory Of Goa

Mormogao

- Major Temples : Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple, Ram Mandir, Ayyappa Temple, Kanteshwarnath Temple
- Major Mosques : Masjid e Fazal, Masjid E Umar
- Major Churches : St. Francis Xavier Church, Our Lady of Candelaria Church
- Forts : Mormugao Fort
- Museums : Indian Naval Aviation Museum, Ashvek Vintage World Car Museum, Bhramlok Museum
- Gardens : Japanese Garden, Vasco Garden

Salcette

- Major Temples : Sai Mandir, Marai Mahalaxmi Temple
- Major Mosques : Masjid-E-Quba, Al Gousia Sunni Masjid And Madrasa , Shiroda Masjid, Masjid-e-Siddique, Arab Sab Sunni Jamia Masjid
- Major Churches : Saviour of the World Church, St. Thomas Church, Church of Our Lady of Remedios, Our Lady of Merces Church, St John The Baptist, Our Lady of Rosary Church, St. Sebastian Church
- Forts : Rachol Historical Fort Gate, Chandor fort, Betul Fort
- Museums : Big Foot Goa, Goa Chitra Museum
- Gardens : Ana Fonte Garden, Municipal Garden Moisal Dam

Sanguem

- Major Temples : Sangameshwar Temple, Paik-Dev Temple, Shree Ramnath Paikdev Temple
- Major Mosques : Noorani Masjid Sanguem Goa, Sunni Jamiya Masjid Curchorem-Goa
- Major Churches : Our Lady of Miracles Church
- Dams : Salaulim Dam
- Gardens : Salaulim Garden Muncipal Garden

Quepem

- Major Temples : Shree Saunsthan Shantadurga Chamundeshwari Kudtari Mahamaya, Chamundeshwari Temple, Mahadev Temple Cotombi Quepem
- Major Mosques : Sunni Jamia Masjid Idgah, Jama Masjid
- Major Churches : Holy Cross Church, St Joseph Chapel, Paroda Church
- Museums : Palácio do Deão
- Dam : Quepem Dam

Canacona

- Major Temples : Shree Gurupradeshi Temple, Nirakar Mandir, Mallikarjun temple, Shri Maruti Mandir
- Major Mosques : Sunni Islamic Jamia Masjid
- Major Churches : St. Anne's Church, Church of St Anthony of Lisbon, St. Sebastian's Church, Agonda Chapel
- Forts : Cabo de Rama Fort
- Sanctuaries : Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary
- Gardens : Chapoli Garden

Dharbandora

- Major Temples : Dhareshwari Temple, Tambdi Surla Temple, Ajoba Temple
- Sanctuary : Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary, Bhagawan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary
