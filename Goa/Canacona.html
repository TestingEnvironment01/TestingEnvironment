Canacona

  1. Overview : Canacona (IPA: [/'kaːɳkɔɳ/]; Goan Konkani: kāṇkŏṇ) is a city and a municipal council in the district of South Goa, Goa state, India. Canacona taluka includes Patnem, Chaudi, Poinguinim, Loliem-Polem, Agonda, and Gaumdongre. Chaudi is the headquarters and the most developed town in this taluka. The famous Palolem Beach is located in Canacona.

  2. Villages and Town in Canacona : List of villages in Canacona taluka : Agonda, Canacona, Cola, Cotigao, Gaodongrem, Loliem, Poinguinim
    - Towns in Canacona : Canacona Municipal Council

  3. Education : The following insitutes are located in Canacona :
    --- Dnyan Prabodhini Mandal's Shree Mallikarjun and Shri Chetan Manju Desai College

  4. Medical Institute : The following medical insititutes are located in Canacona :
    --- Community Health Center, Canacona

  5. History : Canacona was incorporated into Goa in AD 1794. Like most of Goa, the culture of the district reflects an Indo-Portuguese fusion, yet Canacona experienced less Lusitanisation than the central Velhas Conquistas (Old Conquests) of Goa.

  6. Places to Visit : Canacona houses some of the most picturesque beaches in Goa - such as - Cola beach, Agonda beach, Butterfly beach, Cabo de Rama beach (and fort), Talpona beach, Galgibag beach and waterfalls such as teh Bamanbudo waterfalls and Kuskem waterfalls.

    Places to Visit :
    - Major Temples : Shree Gurupradeshi Temple, Nirakar Mandir, Mallikarjun temple, Shri Maruti Mandir
    - Major Mosques : Sunni Islamic Jamia Masjid
    - Major Churches : St. Anne's Church, Church of St Anthony of Lisbon, St. Sebastian's Church, Agonda Chapel
    - Forts : Cabo de Rama Fort
    - Sanctuaries : Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary
    - Gardens : Chapoli Garden
    - Rivers and Ferries : Galjibag River, Talpona River
    - Waterfalls : Sadolxem waterfall
    - Beaches : Palolem Beach, Colomb Beach, Patnem Beach, Rajbag Beach, Talpona Beach, Turtle Beach, Xandrem Beach

  7. Transport : The Canacona bus stand is the last major bus stand before exiting Goa and entering Karwar. This bus stand is well connected between towns in Goa.

    --- Canacona Railway Station
