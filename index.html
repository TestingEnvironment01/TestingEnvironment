Why should you try Sahaja Yoga?

Self Discovery

You cannot know the meaning of your life until you are connected to the power that created you

Verifiable Experience

Every individual irrespective of caste, creed, religion or race can experience the subtle inner energy within each of us

Spontaneous Meditation

It is as simple and natural as breathing. One can almost instantaneously experience a deep silence and an effortless meditative state

Balance In Life

When the Kundalini energy rises, automatically you develop your own balances and that balanced life manifests outside. Sahaja Yoga leads an individual to a spontaneous state of balance.

Better Health

Kundalini cures you, she improves you, she bestows all the blissful things upon you. Sahaja Yoga meditation gives you physical and emotional balance which ultimately improves your health and cure the root cause of problem.

Joy

Joy (ananda) is different from happiness, as happiness is just the state of mind. In joyful state you become thoughtless. Sahaja Yoga is a very subtle happening within us. And this subtler happening gives you sensitivity to divine joy

Ashram Plot

About the Ashram

  1. 1: The ashram would have an area of 5000 square meters, and its dimensions would be 50 meters by 100 meters.

  2. 2: The area would consist of a building at the center-left of the plot, which would be 20 meters by 50 meters, as shown in the pictures

  3. 3: This building would house a total of atleast one thousand Sahajis.

  4. 4: There will be a servant's quarters at the back of the ashram building and two washroom facilities for men and women.

  5. 5: A parking area is also planned which should aid parking facilities for atleast 30 light motor vehicles.

  6. 6: The area surrounding the ashram will be used for plantation of trees such as banana, guavas and certain flowers.
